---
title: Cannot Log In to deepvue
description: Resolve login problems with deepvue.
---

## Symptoms

- Unable to log in
- Password reset not working

## Solution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Email and Password">
    Ensure you are using the correct credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reset Password">
    Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page to reset.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact Support">
    If you still can't access, email support@deepvue.io for help.
  </Step>
</Steps>

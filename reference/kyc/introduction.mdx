## Overview

The KYC Verification APIs enable you to verify the identity of individuals against authoritative government and financial databases. These APIs support real-time validation of identity documents such as PAN cards, bank accounts, passports, and driving licenses, helping you meet regulatory compliance requirements and reduce fraud.

Key capabilities include:

- **PAN verification** with multiple detail levels, Aadhaar linkage checks, and employment status lookups
- **Bank account validation** through penny drop and IFSC verification
- **Async document verification** for driving license, voter ID, and vehicle RC
- **Aadhaar-bank seeding status** checks via OTP-based verification
- **Additional checks** for passport, vehicle RC details, RC challans, name matching, and UPI handle verification

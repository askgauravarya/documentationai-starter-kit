## Overview

The KYB API suite enables you to verify the legitimacy and compliance status of businesses operating in India. These APIs pull data from authoritative government sources to validate tax registrations, corporate filings, MSME credentials, and regulatory licenses in real time.

Use KYB verification to onboard merchants, underwrite business loans, assess supply-chain partners, or satisfy regulatory due-diligence requirements.

## Key capabilities

1. **GST Verification** — Validate GST registrations, retrieve detailed taxpayer profiles, and check return filing status
2. **MCA Corporate Data** — Verify companies and directors through CIN, DIN, and PAN lookups against Ministry of Corporate Affairs records
3. **MSME Validation** — Confirm Udyam and Udyog Aadhaar registrations and track MSME migration status
4. **Tax and Compliance Checks** — Verify TAN details, check TDS 206 compliance, validate FSSAI food-safety licenses, and authenticate Shop Establishment certificates

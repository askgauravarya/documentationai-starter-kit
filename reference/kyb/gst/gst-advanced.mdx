## Overview

This API verifies GST Identification Numbers (GSTINs) for business entities by accessing data registered on the GST Portal. It delivers both fundamental verification details and advanced information points.

The service conducts comprehensive GSTIN verification encompassing:

- Principal and additional contact information (addresses, email addresses, phone numbers)
- Business nature classifications
- Promoter identification
- Annual turnover ranges and fiscal year references
- Cash transaction percentages
- Aadhaar validation status and dates
- Registration and cancellation dates
- Business constitution type (e.g., Public Limited Company)
- Taxpayer classification (Regular status)
- Current GSTIN activation status
- Field inspection completion indicators
- Core business activity codes and descriptions
- Multi-period GST return filing records (GSTR-1, GSTR-3B, GSTR-9, GSTR-9C)
- Jurisdictional information at center and state levels

One Access Token will be valid for only 24 hours. After 24 hours, you can renew the token by using the Authorize Endpoint.

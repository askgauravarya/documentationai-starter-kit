## Overview

DigiLocker is a Digital India Programme initiative designed to eliminate reliance on physical documents by facilitating the secure sharing of verified electronic documents among authorized entities.

## Key advantages

- **Online access** — Share documents from any location, at any time
- **Legally valid** — Digital authentication is comparable to original documents
- **User-friendly** — Simple interface for document access and sharing
- **Direct issuance** — Documents issued by registered authorities including government departments and educational bodies

## Overview

Deepvue's Digilocker KYC APIs enable you to perform Aadhaar-based electronic Know Your Customer (eKYC) verification and retrieve documents directly from DigiLocker. These APIs support real-time identity verification workflows by leveraging India's Aadhaar OTP infrastructure and the DigiLocker digital document ecosystem.

You can choose from three approaches depending on your integration requirements:

- **Offline Aadhaar eKYC (V1)** uses a captcha-based session flow followed by OTP verification. This is the original implementation and provides full control over session management, including captcha reload capabilities.
- **Offline Aadhaar eKYC (V2)** is an enhanced version that removes the captcha requirement, simplifying the integration and reducing friction for end users. OTP generation and verification are handled in two straightforward steps.
- **DigiLocker** integration connects directly to the DigiLocker platform, allowing you to retrieve e-Aadhaar documents, list issued files, and download specific documents through a structured five-step workflow.

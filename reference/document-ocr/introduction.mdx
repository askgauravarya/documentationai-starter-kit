## Overview

The Document OCR APIs enable you to extract text from images and PDF files through optical character recognition (OCR) technology. This automates document processing by identifying and extracting structured data from identity documents.

The system targets fields such as full name, date of birth, and address information found on photo IDs, returning the results in key-value pair format.
